Yellowtail Damsel

Scientific Name: Chrysiptera parasema

Common Name: Yellowtail Damsel

Other Names: Goldtail Demoiselle, Yellowtail Blue Damselfish

Behavior: Semi-Agressive

Habitat:Western Pacific and Western Atlantic oceans (coral & rocky reefs)

Diet: Omnivore

Size: Up to 3 inches

Lifespan: 4-6 years

Reproduction:Males prepare a nest site on dead coral, and the female deposits adhesive eggs on the substrate.

Cool Facts: The male Yellowtail Damselfish becomes extremely territorial and guards the eggs alone
