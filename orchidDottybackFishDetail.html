Orchid Dottyback

Scientific Name: Pseudochromis fridmani

Common Name: Orchid Dottyback

Other Names: Fridman’s dottyback or Fridman’s pseudochromis.

Behavior:Aggressive

Habitat: Coral reefs of the Red Sea, Australia, Asia

Diet: Carnivorous

Size: Up to 3 inches

Lifespan: 5-7 years

Reproduction: Egg-layers that form pairs to reproduce, with the male tending to the demersal eggs laid in a crevice or cave

Cool Facts:This fish camouflages as its prey's parents to trick it into becoming dinner.
