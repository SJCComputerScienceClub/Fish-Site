Clown Fish

Scientific Name: Amphiprion percula

Common Name: Platinum Clownfish

Other Names: Anemone fish

Behavior: Semi-Aggresive

Habitat:Indian, Pacific Oceans, Red Sea

Diet: Omnivore

Size: 3 inches

Lifespan: 5 years

Reproduction: External Fertilization and are all born male

Cool Facts: Can change physiology
