Coral Beauty Angelfish

Scientific Name: Centropyge bispinosa

Common Name: Coral Beauty Angelfishh

Other Names: Twospined, Dusky Angelfish

Behavior: Semi-Aggresive

Habitat:Indo-Pacific, Great Barrier Reef

Diet: Omnivore, primarily algae

Size: 4 inches

Lifespan: 10-15 Years

Reproduction: Male and female will simultaneously release sperm and egg at dusk into the water column

Cool Facts: Is considered one of the more intelligent Angelfish
