Striped Blenny

Scientific Name: Name: Meiacanthus grammistes

Common Name: Striped Blenny

Other Names: Grammistes, Striped Fang, Striped Poison-Fang Blenny

Behavior:Semi-Agressive

Habitat: Western Pacific Ocean

Diet: Omnivore

Size: 5 inches

Lifespan: 3 - 5 years

Reproduction: External fertilization

Cool Facts:Male will fertilize different females and guard all the eggs in a pile
