Indigo Dottyback

Scientific Name: Pseudochromis fridamani x sankeyi

Common Name: Bicolor Foxface

Other Names: None

Behavior: Semi-Agressive

Habitat:Indian Ocean and Red Sea

Diet: Carnivore

Size: 3 inches

Lifespan: 3-5 years

Reproduction: External fertilization with the male guarding the eggs

Cool Facts: Hybrid of Orchid Dottyback and Striped Dottyback
