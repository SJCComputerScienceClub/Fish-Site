Bicolor Foxface

Double Click Window to Toggle 3D Viewing Mode

Common Name: Bicolor Foxface
Scientific Name: Siganus upsi
Adult Size: 7-9 inches, with females being larger than males
Habitat: Western pacific tropical marine reefs around Fiji
Behavior: Diurnal (active during the day) with adults in pairs and juveniles forming schools
Lifespan: 5-7 years
Diet: Omnivore; algae and zooplankton
Reproduction: External fertilization with eggs becoming part of the plankton
Cool Facts: Spines are venomous and used for defense
Other Names: Upsi Spinfoot, Rabbitfish
