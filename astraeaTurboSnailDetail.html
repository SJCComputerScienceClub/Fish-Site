Astraea Turbo Snail

Common Name: Astraea Turbo Snail
Scientific Name: Astraea tecta
Adult Size: 0.5-1.0 inch
Habitat: Tropical areas in the Atlantic Ocean
Behavior: Nocturnal (active at night), peaceful grazer
Lifespan: 1-2 years
Diet: Herbivore; algae
Reproduction: Has difficulty righting itself due to the shape of its shell
Cool Facts: Can change sex
Other names: Star Snail, Conehead Snail
