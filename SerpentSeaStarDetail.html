Serpent Sea Star

Common Name: Brittle Star

Scientific Name: Ophiuroidea

Adult Size: 6 inches to over a foot in diameter

Habitat: Shallow intertidal zones to deep-sea coral reefs

Behavior: Solitary

Lifespan: 3-5 years

Diet: Small organisms

Reproduction: Sexual and asexual methods

Cool Facts: Can regenerate lost limbs

Other Names: Serpent stars or serpent starfish
