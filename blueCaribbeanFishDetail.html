Blue Caribbean Tang

Scientific Name: Acanthurus Coeruleus

Common Name: Blue Caribbean Tang

Other Names: Blue Tang, Blue Surgeonfish, Blue Doctorfish

Behavior: Aggresive

Habitat:Atlantic

Diet: Herbivore

Size: 15 inches

Lifespan: 12-15 years

Reproduction: External fertilization and scatter eggs

Cool Facts: Their caudal spine can cut like a scalpel, gicing them their nicknames
